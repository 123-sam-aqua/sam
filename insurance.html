Why do I need car insurance?

Car insurance is required by law, unless you've registered your vehicle as being off the road.

The minimum level of insurance is third party only, which covers you against liability to third parties following a road traffic accident.

Third party, fire and theft provides the same cover as third party, plus cover in the event of your car being stolen or damaged by fire.
